Cat Blood Type Guide

Understanding feline blood compatibility

A

Type A

The most common blood type. Type A cats can safely receive blood from other Type A donors.

Can donate to A Cannot donate to B Caution with AB
B

Type B

Less common overall but prevalent in certain breeds. Type B cats have strong anti-A antibodies.

Can donate to B Cannot donate to A Caution with AB
AB

Type AB

The rarest feline blood type. AB cats are universal recipients and can receive from A, B, or AB donors.

Can receive from A, B, AB Should only donate to AB

⚠️ Important

Always consult a veterinarian before any blood transfusion. Cross-matching tests should be performed even between same-type cats.
